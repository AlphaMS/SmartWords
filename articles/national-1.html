UNEMPLOYMENT- A MAJOR CHALLENGE FOR INDIA

- By Mihir Gandhi
November 14,2021

One of the major issue of the developing countries like “india” is the unemployment. The main reason behind this is the lack of skills and knowledge.

India’s population is the Main concern behind it that the availability of jobs is limited and the no of applicants are too much Who are sending their profiles for job is more so because of this reason unemployment ratio is increasing every day? India is standing at no 86 position in unemployment rate Study in a world with a rating of 8.5%. To overcome this and to provide job Opportunities new startups, companies, and establishment of industries are done by the central government.

More focus should be given to startup’s because a startup can provide more job Opportunities for all the skilled people who have done their courses on a particular Domain. Also, the main reason behind unemployment is the literacy rate of india that is Only 70% so there is lack of skill so they did not get the job opportunities. As we are Leading to a digital world so work of 100 persons are done by 1 machine so this leads in cut in job necessity. Machines are taking the role of persons.

The government of india has Also, some schemes like

1. Mnrega under which the unemployed person can get a job for 100 for days in a year.

2. Dday which aims to provide skill training and skill Upgrading and providing paying job opportunities to the unemployed person. Skill training and startups are introduced in the subjects in the indian school text Book for the overall development of a child. National education policy will also help The backward classes of india for improving their knowledge and skill by various Programmes. Training campaign has been run to aware the people about the Importance of education and skills improvement. In the coming next few years, the unemployment ratio will decrease because so many Big companies are coming to india for establishing their factories; they are choosing India.
