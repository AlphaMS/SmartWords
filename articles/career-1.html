What to do after 10th Boards?

- By Mihir Gandhi
November 14,2021

What to do after 10 board?

In which field should I go? Which stream is good? Should I go for science stream or commerce or diploma? So hello everyone, today we are going to talk on what should be our next stream after 10. I am going to guide you for which field you should select after 10.

So, let’s, get started. After 10 board what should I select for my career?

Many students take science after 10 and in starting they are studying good but as time goes their interest become less and afterwards in future they feel regret of taking a wrong decision. So choosing a correct field after 10 / to choose a correct career is very important. So let me tell you that choosing a good career is toughest and easy to find out. Why I am saying that it is toughest and easy, because the main thing you want to know is the interest in which field you feel comfortable and easy to study it for long time without any hesitation. The tough thing is that you have to find the interest and the easy thing that it will be easy after finding interest to choose the correct career/ stream.

How can I find my own interest?

To find out the interest you have to ask your mind that in which thing you feel satisfied and comfortable and works towards it for longtime. Here many people get confused between interest and hobby so it is necessary to understand what interest is and hobby and what the difference between them is? So let us understand the difference between interest and hobby.

Difference between interest and hobby

So basically interest is like you want to do something and you want to work on it for the whole life and you are eagerly ready to do that work. Now hobby is totally depends person to person some people like to play cricket some like to play football so it depends person to person. It can be as many as you want.so hobby is the thing/work you want to do in free work.

Hobby can become interest if we take hobby as a proffesion.it can be said that interest is a subset of hobby. So now, hopefully you might be knowing the difference between interest and hobby.

Now after finding your interest in which thing you feel interesting then you have to go ahead in that field and find more knowledge of that field so that you can see that you are on correct path of your interest. Let me explain through you an example: Suppose you want to become an engineer then you have to search and find out all type of field i.e. all type of engineers that your fiel contains and should have basic knowledge of it.After finding basic knowledge of the engineering field then you can choose that you should go for math stream and now your goal should be focused on getting good marks in JEE and boards and to get admission in good college.

Basic knowledge of all stream:

1. Science

In science there are two paths either you can go for biology or math. Biology: If you take biology then you can become a doctor, a highly educated professor pharmaceutical job, a nurse and many more. For becoming doctor you have to prepare for NEET exam which you can apply and get admission in high college and become doctor of the field you want to become e.g.: MBBS, dentist, Ayurveda and many more… Math: If you go for math you can become an engineer, a highly educated professor and many more. For getting admission in good college you have to scored good in JEE Main and boards both because both marks will be required to get admission in college.Thorugh JEE you can go in top colleges of India like IIT,NIT,IIIT and many more…

2. Commerce: In commerce you can become CA, bank manager etc. and this field is so wide just like science

3. Diploma: You can do diploma and can directly do engineering after diploma without studying in 11-12.You can become engineer and in this field.

NOTE: Hard work must be required to whatever you select the stream as a career because without hard work you cannot success. So hard work will be achieved only and only if you have interest in that stream. Thanks for reading the article, best of luck for your future choose the correct career and have a bright future.
